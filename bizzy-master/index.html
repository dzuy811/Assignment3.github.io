OVERVIEW

Due to the fact that, there are so many fatal diseases that could impact on people negatively. I want to develop a project called “Nutrition and Fitness” in the future to help people handle this problem. Nutrition and Fitness is a web that is available on both PC and mobile phones is easily used. In short, this web is used to balance the number of nutrition which people consume and support for people who are doing gymnastics. Moreover, Nutrition and Fitness also suggests for customers many options that they can reduce obesity and heart disease. It is a good option for people who want to lose weight because Nutrition and Fitness are able to calculate the amount of calories that people consume to release the best solution for customers.

    MOTIVATION


    Health plays an important role in human lives. Nonetheless, people tend to ignore their health because of the development of urban life. People are prone to consume unhealthy food in order to save time because they do not have time for cooking, which leads to an increase of heart disease and some relevant issues. For example, the University of Paris reported that there are approximately 100.000 people who consume unhealthy food every day had worse heart disease. Using Nutrition and Fitness is a cheap and easy way to reduce heart disease. Moreover, this web also guides people on how to release or reduce nutrition so they can avoid overweight and obesity, and have better health status.

    TOOLS


    Initially, the user should have a mobile phone in order to install and set up the web. After that, customers can use their own Gmail or facebook or twitter to sign up.

    Software: Using in website or users can download it as software in personal computer

    Hardware: high-tech mobile phone so that the web can run smoothly.

    SKILLS


    Software: Programming languages must be known to build a web which operates on both IOS and Android. For instance, they are C#, Java, Python, Swift, and C++.

    Maintenance: Problem-solving is the most significant skill to run and develop this website.

    Fitness: Having a profound knowledge of physical health and nutrition especially about gymnastics and balanced diet.

    OUTCOME


    If this project is successful, I believe that the number of people who have heart disease and obesity would be dramatically declined. Moreover, after using this web, customers should pay attention to their health as well as their family. From my perspective, people who are using this web would improve their health and share it with everyone so that the number of death could significantly decrease.
